What is a paid post?

Paid posts allow sponsors to reach The Texas Tribune’s policy-minded audience through brand messaging in the form of written articles. The points of view expressed don’t reflect those of the Tribune and sponsors play no role in the Tribune’s editorial decisions. The Texas Tribune is the only member-supported, digital-first, nonpartisan media organization that informs Texans — and engages with them — about public policy, politics, government and statewide issues.
