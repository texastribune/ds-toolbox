Helper classes needed

Note that we also sprinkle in sibling helper classes with story-body. For example, the t-linkstyle helper gives us the ability to style links like this.

  • has-text-black-off
  • t-linkstyle
  • t-linkstyle--underlined
  • has-l-btm-marg
  • t-serif
  • t-space-heading-m

Our editor has a dropdown option called Subheader for adding a heading element. By default, that tag is an h3. In the event someone were to open the source and add others, this is how they would display.
